About Me

Hello, my name is William. Since I was a young boy, I have been greatly fascinated by the world of computers, especially by the video games of my childhood and the mechanics behind complex systems. That passion led me to become a programmer, eventually earning my degree in Computer Science.

What I enjoy most is building robust and reliable software, such as databases, SaaS, web apps, and games. Whether working in a team or independently, I love making things work and solving real-world problems through code.

My natural curiosity has driven me to research various languages, paradigms, and fields. This has allowed me to develop a versatile skillset, involving myself in projects like backend services for my university, game engines built from scratch, and modern frontend web development.

I firmly believe in the value of teamwork and clear communication to create high-quality software. For me, every project is an opportunity to improve my toolset, mindset, and professional skills.
